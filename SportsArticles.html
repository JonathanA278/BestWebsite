Tyler Passmore Takes His Talents to NJIT, Commits to Division 1 Track Program

Freehold, NJ — After an outstanding high school track career, Freehold Borough senior Tyler Passmore is set to take his athletic journey to the next level, securing a Division 1 scholarship with the NJIT Highlanders. Passmore, who graduated this spring, has been a standout sprinter for the Freehold Boro track team, and his commitment to NJIT marks a significant milestone in his budding career. Throughout his high school tenure, Passmore consistently impressed with his speed and determination, earning multiple medals in both county and state competitions. His versatility across multiple sprinting events, including the 200m and 400m, made him one of the team’s most reliable athletes and a leader on the track. "I’m beyond excited to continue my track career at NJIT," Passmore said. "It’s been a dream of mine to compete at the Division 1 level, and I’m grateful for the support I’ve had from my coaches, teammates, and family. Freehold Boro has been a huge part of my growth, and I’m ready to represent them on a bigger stage." His future coaches at NJIT have high expectations for Passmore, with many noting his exceptional work ethic and natural talent. "Tyler’s dedication to his craft and his competitive drive make him a perfect fit for our program," said [Coach's Name], head coach of the NJIT track team. "We’re excited to see how he continues to develop as an athlete at the Division 1 level." Passmore’s commitment to NJIT is a testament to his years of hard work and the foundation built by his coaches and teammates at Freehold Boro. As he prepares for the next chapter in his athletic career, the Freehold community is proud to see one of its own continue to excel on a national stage. The Highlanders track team is set to begin their season this fall, and Passmore is ready to put in the miles as he looks to make an immediate impact in the competitive world of collegiate athletics.
